Language Arts

The course is designed to provide high academics while instilling a love for learning in children. It focuses on teaching literature, writing, grammar, punctuation, spelling, vocabulary, geography, and art. The curriculum is faith-based and aims to cultivate good character in students. The course takes small incremental steps to prevent overwhelm and allows parental participation if desired. It goes beyond Common Core standards, emphasizing "good and beautiful" style writing and includes additional materials like index cards, timer, drawing pencils, sketchbook, and a notebook with lined paper. Access to a computer is required for watching videos and typing some writing assignments.
